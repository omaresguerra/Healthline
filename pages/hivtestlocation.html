HIV TEST LOCATION

 

Recommended Testing Sites
1.   Love Yourself Anglo (Shaw Blvd., Mandaluyong City)
The Love Yourself Anglo is open all days of the week except Mondays and Tuesdays. HIV test is free-of-charge. Staff are warm, friendly, and non-discriminatory.

It is located at Unit 5, 3/F, Anglo Building, #715-A Shaw Boulevard, Mandaluyong City. (It is a 3-minute walk from Shaw MRT Station, and is situated just behind Star Mall EDSA). It is at the 3rd floor of a yellow building with Anglo Hardware at the ground floor.

Phone:
  • 0915-366-LOVE (0915-366-5683)

Clinic hours:
  • Wednesdays to Saturdays
    12 NN TO 7PM

  • Sundays
    9 AM TO 2 PM

  • Monday and Tuesday
    CLOSED

*CUT OFF -- time when the last client can be tested so the results can be released on the same day.


2.   Love Yourself Uni (Taft Ave., Pasay City)
Love Yourself Uni is open all days of the week except Mondays and Tuesdays. Staff are also warm, friendly, and non-discriminatory, and the HIV test is free-of-charge.

It is located at 2028 Taft Avenue Extension, Pasay City. The clinic is located conveniently close to LRT-1 Gil Puyat or Buendia station on the northbound lane of Taft Avenue.

There is a Mercury Drug Store at the ground floor of the building.

    CLOSED

*CUT OFF -- time when the last client can be tested so the results can be released on the same day.


3.   Love Yourself Uni (Taft Ave., Pasay City)
The LoveYourself Hub originally located along San Marcelino Street in Malate, Manila is now closed. This clinic has now been replaced by the LoveYourself Uni located in Pasay, details of which have been mentioned above.
